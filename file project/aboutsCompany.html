VIETNAM DAIRY MILK CO., LTD: 8 YEARS - A DEVELOPMENT ROAD

To develop a system of professional dairy farms in accordance with international standards and the provisions of Vietnamese law. Quality products, food safety and hygiene with reasonable price reasonable, close to the average production cost of the world. Production and business activities are practically socio-economic. Vinamilk currently has five dairy farms, and four dairy farms are under construction The above is the main content of the "Management Policy" of Vietnam Dairy Cow One-Member Co., Ltd. The company was established at the time Vinamilk acquired Phu Lam high-calf cattle breeding center is managed by Tuyen Quang Provincial People's Committee, Vinamilk has identified from the beginning that this unit plays a pivotal role in producing and supplying fresh cow's milk to supply. granted to processing plants. The company was granted the business registration certificate by Tuyen Quang Department of Planning and Investment for the first time on December 14, 2006. Activities of the company are quite diversified, including: dairy farming; Cultivation and mixed farming; Production of animal feed, poultry and aquatic products; Wholesaling agricultural and forestry raw materials and live animals; Agents (cattle, veterinary medicine, animal feed, ...). Initially, the farm had a total of 1,400 dairy cows with a total of 92 employees, based in: Dam Khe Hamlet, Phu Lam Commune, Yen Son District, Tuyen Quang Province. Up to now, the chartered capital of the company has reached 1,550 billion VND with the total number of staffs up to 480 people. This shows the encouraging maturation of the unit after 8 years of effort to perform the task. Mr. Trinh Quoc Dung - Managing Director of Material Resources Development EIGHTY - EVERYTHING With the establishment of Vietnam Dairy Cow One Member Limited Company, Vinamilk has initiated a program to develop dairy cattle. In July 2007, the head office of the company was moved to Ho Chi Minh City for the convenience of management. Phu Lam dairy farm was transformed into a subsidiary of Vietnam Dairy One Member Limited Company, with the new name of Tuyen Quang Dairy Farm. Mr. Tran Duc Luong - former President and provincial leaders Visiting and working at Tuyen Quang Dairy Farm. In April 2008, the second dairy farm of the company was Binh Dinh Dairy Farm, which is licensed by the Department of Planning and Investment of Binh Dinh province. The dairy farming in Binh Dinh went into operation, contributing to the supply of raw milk to serve the production needs of Vinamilk's factories in the central region. In 09/2009, the event of Nghe An Dairy Farm inaugurated and put into operation was voted by the media and media as the best farm in 2014 (*), with the total capital of Vinamilk Initial investment is over 100 billion. In July 2010, Vinamilk acquired all its shares in Lam Son Dairy Products Joint Stock Company, including Sao Vang Dairy Farm. Vinamilk then handed over this farm to Vietnam Dairy One Member Limited Company for management. In August 2010, the Department of Planning and Investment of Thanh Hoa province granted the operation license and officially renamed this farm as Thanh Hoa Dairy Farm. In April 2012, Lam Dong dairy farm completed construction of breeding facilities and imported cattle. This is the fifth dairy farm of the company was put into operation. In December 2013, the Department of Planning and Investment of Tay Ninh province has granted operation permit to Tay Ninh Dairy Farm, the farm with the size of 8,000 dairy cows located in Ben Cau district (Tay Ninh province) The office of Tay Ninh Dairy Farm was built on an area of ​​685 ha In 2014 and the next period, the company will continue to implement new farm projects such as Ha Tinh dairy farm (scale 2,000) in Huong Son district, Ha Tinh province; Thanh Hoa 2 dairy cow farm (scale of 2,000 heads) in Nhu Thanh district, Thanh Hoa province; and the Thanh Nhat Thong Nhat Dairy Co., Ltd. with a size of 16,000 were built on an area of ​​nearly 2,500 ha in Yen Dinh district, Thanh Hoa province. MODERN TECHNOLOGY EQUIPMENT INVESTMENT At present, all dairy cows system is invested by the company with the most modern technology in the world. As: roof systems are applied cold-resistant technology with cold insulation layers; automatic scraping system; automatic drinking trough system; Cooling system in the cage. Sleeping cells for cows were lined with rubber latex imported from Sweden to ensure that their feet are clean and free of infection. The cows and shelters of the herd are equipped with an automatic itching system. Each cow was given an electronic chip under the collar for identification through the modern Alpro system provided by Delaval. These electronic chips help to check the milk accurately for each individual and to detect cows and estrus
